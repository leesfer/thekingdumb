Who is "The Kingdumb"?


The Kingdumb is a Southern California based DJ and producer group who, in the last year, has pushed and broken the boundaries of dubstep, bass, moombahton, and has edged along creating their own genre known as "kingstep." The Kingdumb obliterates dancefloors with their heavy bass and genre-destroying sound that keep their ridiculous shows in a storm of twerking and sweat. The Kingdumb knows how to bring out the crazy party person in all of us, directing the crowd from the stage in their own clowd of energy and they do it via their signature sets, specifically playing only their own studio creations.

The Kingdumb might be one of the craziest live acts in the EDM scene right now, but they are also known by those in the know as some of the hardest working producers, constantly trying new things and experimenting with new sound to push their "kingstep" to new heights. If they are not playing a set, they are guaranteed to be in the studio from morning to dusk. Collectively, they've been everywhere over the past few years, releasing music on every EDM label you can think of. Between Anjunabeats, Toolroom, Spinnin', Buygore, Firepower, Sweetshop, Ride, Enhanced, Zero3, SPRS, Atlantic, and Ultra, The Kingdumb has an incredibly diverse background of production, and it shows in everything they release.
